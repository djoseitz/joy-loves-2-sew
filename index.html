About Me

Sewing is a wonderful hobby. Fabrics today are so beautiful and interesting. I love to put colors and designs together and create something new! I strive for a contemporary and fun style, making each piece unique and one of a kind.

Over the last several years I’ve shown my work at local craft shows. It’s great to meet people and be part of the “makers” community. As items sell, it inspires me to keep on creating!!

This site serves as a display for you to view and enjoy. If something catches your eye, of course, I’m happy to arrange a purchase! Visit the Contact page to learn more:
