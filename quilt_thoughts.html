My Thoughts on Quilts

Since I started making lap quilts, I’ve come to believe that they are unique… something much more than a functional item. They do not serve to match your decor, instead they provide a statement of their own. They are labors of love and are meant to stand the test of time and be enjoyed for years to come.

I find quilts in particular to be a great way to make a statement and give a personalized gift to someone special. Below are various lap quilts I’ve made for friends. They celebrate special occasions and friendship.

If you are looking to give a special gift to someone, perhaps a lap quilt would be the answer! I’m happy to work with you and create something personal together. Feel free to leave me a message on my Contact Page.
